Who We Are

Unity Circus Rwanda is a group that professionally devotes itself to the genre of Acrotheatre. The professional approach to theatre genres and circus disciplines is an important aspect to delivering the messages that spotlight what causes pain, and anxieties amongst the world population and turning them around to joy, peace, love, reconciliation through Acrotheatre.

Support Us

Help us spread a passion for the arts across Rwanda. We believe that all children, regardless of their background, deserve to have access to the healing powers of the arts. Our artistic training provides art therapy as well as professional skills that lead to lasting employment. Your donation directly supports these programs.

New & Our Story

Unity Circus and the Covid 19 Pandemic

As the world faces the COVID-19 (Coronavirus) pandemic, the Government of Rwanda today is confirming different cases of infected persons in most of the district in that country.

Training children in circus /Acrotheatre

Many children of all ages from the rural areas around Gisenyi come to Unity Circus to have Circus training as their freetime activity and to express their feelings through acrotheatre.

Encouraging young artist to attend their schools

Many children in rural areas of Rwanda don't attend schools. The reasons are that they are not able to afford schoolfees and that they don't perform well so they loose motivation.

Food distribution during the time of calamity

Food distribution during times of calamity is a critical aspect of emergency response efforts. Ensuring that affected populations have access to an adequate and nutritious food supply is essentialfortheir survival .
